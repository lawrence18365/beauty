01 Shaping — Tinting — Lamination

Brows

Brow work starts with shape, balance and the small details that make everything sit properly. Ciara maps each brow to your features so the result feels polished, flattering and still like you.

Lashes

Lash lifts and tints are designed to bring out your natural lashes rather than cover them up. The focus is clean lift, good separation and an easy finish that opens the eye.
