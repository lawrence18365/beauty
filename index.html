Award-Winning Artist

The Artist Behind the Vision

Beauty is more than just an appearance — it's about confidence, empowerment, and transformation.

Originally from Ireland, Ciara's passion for beauty was ingrained from a young age. Her journey took her from a small town to the global stage, representing Ireland at WorldSkills — one of the most prestigious international competitions in the world.

Competing in China and Abu Dhabi, she placed 3rd in the world for Best Beautician in 2017. But beyond the accolades, what truly shaped her was the unwavering belief of her trainer — someone who saw her potential before she ever did.
