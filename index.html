01 / About
A little about me

I like beauty to feel polished, never overdone

I have never been interested in doing the most. I care about good work, soft finishes and making women feel comfortable, confident and well looked after.

I'm originally from Ireland and have spent more than eight years in beauty. Representing Ireland at WorldSkills taught me discipline, consistency and the value of getting the small things right.

After placing 3rd in the world for Best Beautician in 2017, I brought that same standard into every treatment and every course I teach in Sydney. Whether you're booking in for brows, lashes, permanent makeup or training, you'll be in very good hands.
