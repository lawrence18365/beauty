Why Ciara Teaches

The story behind the academy

Good training should leave you clearer, steadier and more confident in your work — not overwhelmed.

Ciara knows what it is like to grow through good guidance. The belief of her own trainer helped shape her career, and that same standard of care now runs through every course she teaches.

Whether it is a focused treatment course or one-to-one mentoring, the aim is simple: solid technique, thoughtful feedback and training you can actually use with clients.
