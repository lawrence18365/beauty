Why I teach

I care deeply about good training

When I teach, I want you to leave feeling clearer, steadier and far more confident in what you're doing.

My own career was shaped by good guidance. Representing Ireland at WorldSkills taught me discipline, and having a trainer who really believed in me changed everything.

That is the standard I bring into my academy now in Sydney: careful teaching, real technique and support that is actually useful once you're back with clients.
