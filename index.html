01 / About
Meet Ciara

The story behind the studio

Good beauty work should feel like you — just more polished, more confident, and more considered.

Originally from Ireland, Ciara built her career on discipline, detail and a real love for the craft. Representing Ireland at WorldSkills took her to the global stage and shaped the standard she still works to today in Sydney.

After placing 3rd in the world for Best Beautician in 2017, she brought that same calm precision into every treatment and every course she teaches. The focus is simple: natural-looking results, thoughtful guidance, and work done properly.
